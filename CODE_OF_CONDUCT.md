# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity,
or experience level. This is a technical project in a regulated field; we value
rigor, transparency, and respectful disagreement about the numbers.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and statistical opinions.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive, evidence-based feedback.
- Focusing on what is correct and clear for the reader (a reviewer or sponsor).

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project
maintainers via a private issue or the contact listed in the repository. All
complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
